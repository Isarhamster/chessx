Q: Where do I get the latest version of ChessX?

A: You may download it form the project page at sourceforge: http://newscid.sourceforge.net/

Q: What is SCID and how ist ChessX related to it?

A: ChessX is based on SCID which has been developed by Shane Hudson (http://scid.sourceforge.net/). Due to personal reasons from Shane Hudson development of SCID halted. To fill this gap ChessX has been started.
